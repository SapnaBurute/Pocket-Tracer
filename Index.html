Pocket Tracer

Your personal finance assistant that helps you manage your money better

Pocket Tracer is a smart and simple app that helps you keep track of your daily expenses and budget. Whether you want to save more, spend less, or just have a better overview of your money, Pocket Tracer can help you achieve your financial goals. With Pocket Tracer

Easily record your income and expenses in various categories, such as food, transport, entertainment, etc.Quickly view your monthly usage and total expenses in a neat and clear table.Analyze your spending habits and patterns with colorful graphs and charts.

Set your monthly budget and get notified when you are close to or exceed your limit.

Sync your data across multiple devices and access it anytime, anywhere.
